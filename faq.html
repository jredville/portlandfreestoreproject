Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about visiting and donating

We offer donated goods at no cost to the community. Our goals are to increase access and affordability to everyday items, reduce waste, and build stronger community connections.
We are passionate about no-cost goods for everyone!
Yes! All donated goods are looking for new homes, and will be free of charge.
Our space is open to everyone. We hope to reduce the stigma around secondhand goods and encourage the whole community to participate. No proof of income — or anything else — is required.
By encouraging reuse over disposal, we save usable goods from ending up in a landfill. This saves energy, materials, water, and reduces emissions.
Inventory varies widely but generally you can find clothing, housewares, some non-perishable foods, kids' clothing and toys.
All items are donated by community members and local partners who want their gently used goods to continue being useful rather than thrown away.
We take gently used clothing and houseware items, some craft supplies, unopened non-perishable foods, bags, backpacks, and unopened hygiene products. We are unable to take furniture at this time due to space. We can accept one bag or box per person at each visit. Thank you!
We are supported by community donations, small fundraisers, and grants. We are completely volunteer-run.
