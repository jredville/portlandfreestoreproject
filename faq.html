Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about visiting and donating

Visiting the Store

No. Everything in the store is completely free. We do not accept money for any items. If you'd like to support us financially, you can donate via Venmo or our donation page — but it is never required.
No ID, no income verification, no paperwork of any kind. Everyone is welcome to take what they need, no questions asked.
Take what you need. We ask that you be considerate of others who may need the same items, but there are no strict limits. If you have a specific need (like furnishing an apartment), reach out to us in advance and we'll do our best to help.
We are open Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Saturday, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on Mondays and Fridays. Hours may occasionally vary — follow us on Instagram or TikTok for any changes.
We strive to make the store as accessible as possible. Please contact us with any specific accessibility questions or needs and we will do our best to accommodate you.

Donating Items

We accept gently used clothing (all sizes and genders), shoes, household goods, kitchenware, bedding, books, and toys. Items should be clean, functional, and something you would give to a friend. See our Store Policies page for the full list of what we do and don't accept.
Donations are accepted during store hours: Tues–Thurs and Sat–Sun, 10 AM–5 PM. Please do not leave donations outside when the store is closed — items left unattended can be damaged and create extra work for our volunteers.
Yes! We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. We can provide a donation receipt for in-kind contributions. Ask a volunteer at the time of your donation or contact us afterward.
Space at the store is limited, so we ask that you contact us before bringing furniture or large items. We'll let you know if we have room and whether we can accept your specific item.

Volunteering & Getting Involved

We love volunteers! Send us a message through the Contact page or reach out on Instagram. We'll connect you with our volunteer coordinator to find a shift that works for you. No experience necessary — just enthusiasm.
Yes! We welcome community partnerships for donation drives, volunteer groups, and sponsorships. Get in touch and let's talk about how we can work together.

Still have questions? We're happy to help.
