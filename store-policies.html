Store Policies

Guidelines that keep the store welcoming for everyone

Shopping Policies

  • Everything is free — no money is exchanged for any item.
  • No ID, income verification, or referral is required to shop.
  • Take what you need. Be mindful of others who may need the same items.
  • Children are welcome and must be accompanied by an adult.
  • Please treat volunteers and other shoppers with respect.
  • No reselling of items taken from the store.
  • If you have a large or urgent need (e.g., furnishing a home after a disaster), please reach out to us before visiting so we can best help you.

What We Accept

  • Gently used clothing — all sizes, all genders, all ages
  • Shoes in good condition (clean, no major damage)
  • Coats, jackets, and outerwear
  • Bedding and linens (washed and in good condition)
  • Towels and washcloths
  • Kitchen items: dishes, cookware, utensils, small appliances
  • Books (any genre, including children's books)
  • Toys and games (complete sets preferred; clean and safe)
  • Backpacks and bags
  • Hygiene and personal care products (unopened)
  • Household cleaning supplies (unopened)
  • Non-perishable food items (not expired)
  • Art supplies
  • Electronics in working condition

What We Cannot Accept

  • Items with stains, tears, or significant damage
  • Items that smell of smoke, mildew, or strong odors
  • Mattresses or upholstered furniture (due to bed bug risk)
  • Car seats (cannot verify safety history)
  • Cribs or baby sleep products (safety standards may be outdated)
  • Hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, batteries)
  • Opened or expired food or personal care items
  • Adult-only materials
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Items that are broken and cannot be repaired

Not sure if we can take something? Ask us before bringing it in.

Donation Drop-Off

  • Donations are accepted during open hours only: Tues–Thurs and Sat–Sun, 10 AM–5 PM.
  • Please do not leave donations outside when the store is closed.
  • Items should be clean and ready to go on the floor immediately.
  • Please sort donations by category if possible (clothing, books, etc.).
  • Large items or furniture must be arranged in advance — contact us first.
  • Tax receipts are available for in-kind donations. Ask a volunteer.

Volunteer & Visitor Conduct

  • We are a welcoming space for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or circumstance.
  • Discriminatory language or behavior will not be tolerated and may result in being asked to leave.
  • Please help keep the store tidy — fold clothing, put items back where you found them.
  • Volunteers have the right to refuse service to anyone who is disruptive or unsafe.
