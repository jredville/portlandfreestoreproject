Store Policies

Guidelines that keep the store welcoming for everyone

Code of Conduct

  • Be Kind: No hurting, yelling, or touching others.
  • Share: Leave some for others.
  • Keep it clean: Refold or replace items when possible.
  • Watch kids: Keep your children with you.

If Someone Breaks the Rules

  • First time: We will remind you of the rules.
  • Second time: We will ask you to leave for the day.
  • If dangerous: You cannot come back to the store.

What We Accept

  • Gently used clothing and houseware items
  • Craft supplies
  • Unopened non-perishable foods
  • Bags and backpacks
  • Unopened hygiene products
  • Kids' clothing and toys

We can accept one bag or box per person at each visit. Thank you!

What We Cannot Accept

  • Furniture (we don't have the space at this time)
  • Items with significant damage, stains, or strong odors
  • Opened or expired food or personal care items

Not sure? Ask us before bringing it in.
