About Us

Community-powered, volunteer-run, always free

Our Mission

Our journey began with a TikTok post from the founder of Enough Stuff, a free store in Illinois. We were each inspired to connect and work on turning this shared vision into a reality.

Our mission is to provide individuals and families with access to essential goods, free of charge, in a welcoming and supportive environment. We are committed to creating an equitable space while challenging the culture of overconsumption.

We are completely volunteer-run and supported by community donations, small fundraisers, and grants. Everything in the store is free — always.

What We Believe

🤝

Mutual Aid

We're neighbors helping neighbors — not charity. Everyone who uses the store is also welcome to contribute to it.

🌿

Sustainability

Extending the life of goods keeps them out of landfills. Reuse is the most sustainable option.

💛

Dignity

No income verification. No referrals. No embarrassment. Everyone shops the same way.

The People Behind the Store

Volunteers making it happen every week

Jess

President

Jess has a background in accounting and working with nonprofits, and a north node in Aquarius. Starting a free store in Portland seemed like an amazing way to affirm her beliefs in the abundance of our community's resources and their equitable distribution. When she's not filing 990s, Jess enjoys reading, talking to her plants and feeding her 5 cats.

Rachel

Director / Treasurer

Rachel joined after moving to Oregon in summer 2024, when she was searching for free resources herself. With a background in project management and customer service, she's passionate about access, equity, and building community. Outside of work, Rachel loves yoga and hiking.

Amarie

Secretary

Amarie joined in 2024 with a passion for ensuring our community has access to the resources we all need. She believes in the power of mutual support and the value of repurposing to uplift one another. Outside of this work, Amarie loves spending time in nature, gardening, and building meaningful connections with others.
