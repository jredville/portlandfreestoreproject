Our Mission

The Portland Free Store Project exists to reduce waste and increase access to essential goods by connecting neighbors with one another through the gift economy. We believe that everyone deserves dignity, and that communities thrive when we share what we have.

Founded in 2020 in response to the urgent needs of our neighborhood, we've grown from a small sidewalk free box into a full storefront serving hundreds of community members every month. Everything in our store is free — always has been, always will be.

We accept donations of gently used clothing, household goods, books, and more. We are entirely funded by the generosity of individual donors and community partners. No government grants, no corporate sponsors — just neighbors helping neighbors.

What We Believe

🤝

Mutual Aid

We're neighbors helping neighbors — not charity. Everyone who uses the store is also welcome to contribute to it.

🌿

Sustainability

Extending the life of goods keeps them out of landfills. Reuse is the most sustainable option.

💛

Dignity

No income verification. No referrals. No embarrassment. Everyone shops the same way.
